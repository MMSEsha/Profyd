# Profyd
Profyd is a Pakistan-based hiring platform that connects talented tutors and professionals with international opportunities—ethically, transparently, and with zero recruitment fees. 
Profyd is a Pakistan-based hiring platform focused on connecting skilled professionals with structured, long-term career opportunities. The company primarily specializes in recruiting in-office tutors in Lahore for international students, while also hiring for corporate and technical roles.

Profyd operates with a strong foundation in ethical recruitment, transparency, and merit-based hiring. It does not charge candidates any recruitment fees and follows a structured evaluation system to ensure quality placements.

Recruitment Process

Profyd follows a structured recruitment process designed to maintain quality and fairness.

Step 1: Application Submission

Candidates apply through the official website.

Step 2: Initial Screening

The HR team reviews qualifications, experience, and relevance.

Step 3: Assessment

Applicants may be required to complete subject tests or skill assessments.

Step 4: Interview

Shortlisted candidates attend interviews to evaluate communication, expertise, and cultural fit.

Step 5: Final Selection

Successful candidates receive formal job offers.

The entire process is merit-based and transparent.
